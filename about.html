Dairy products are foods or beverages made from the milk of mammals, most commonly cows, but also goats, sheep, and others. They include a wide variety of products, such as milk, cheese, yogurt, butter, cream, and ice cream. These products are rich in nutrients like calcium, protein, vitamins (such as B12), and minerals (such as potassium).
