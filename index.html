About Us

KRISHNA DAIRY  THE BEST DAIRY PRODUCT

Welcome to Krishna Dairy, your trusted source for the finest dairy products in Nagpur. Located in the heart of Nagpur, Krishna Dairy was founded in 2007 by Mr. Prakash, whose vision was to bring the freshest and highest quality dairy products to our community at affordable prices.

Dedicated Services
At Krishna Dairy, we specialize in the sale and manufacture of a wide range of dairy products, all crafted with the utmost care and dedication. Our commitment to freshness means that our products are made daily, ensuring that you receive the best quality with every purchase.
Original Products
Over the years, Krishna Dairy has built a loyal customer base that spans across Nagpur. Our customers trust us for our consistent quality, friendly service, and dedication to their needs. Whether you are looking for fresh milk, rich butter, creamy yogurt, or any other dairy delight, Krishna Dairy is your go-to destination.
